In the Name of Convenience

I have formulated possible three solutions that my allow us to move on from this pandemic. Though only one of these is viable in my opinion. The first would be to Coordinate and Crush the virus. This plan would work through the coordination of all nations to synchronously starve the virus by enforcing strict isolation and distancing rules to starve the virus all around the world. This solution would be the most effective but the major issue with this plan is that it requires nearly impossible global cooperation to function. If a few individuals do not cooperate, they may spread the virus all over again. The second solution would be to delay the virus and buy time to research a vaccine. This solution is what current leaders are trying to do. But as you can see right now, it seems to be very inefficient. Corruption, indolence, and the lack of education has plagued humans longer than any virus has. The power system is broken, the poor are being forced to work whilst the rich do not acknowledge their privilege and protest against the regulations of the lockdown. The people need to learn a lesson.

This brings us to the third solution, my proposal. I say that we should barge through this virus like a medieval batting ram plowing through the gates of an enemy castle. The plan is simpler than any other yet will be much more effective. For in my proposal, I suggest that all local governments remove all isolation and distancing regulations, allowing everyone to mingle again in crowds and be exposed to the virus. This would allow herd immunity to naturally kick in eventually. In my proposal, theoretically, 99% of the population needs to be exposed; an estimated 15% of the population get severely sick, 35% of the population die and go through mass cremation, while the rest are either asymptomatic or recover slowly and gain immunity. The casualties are a necessary setback for the progression of humanity through this pandemic and in general. The indolent and the poor shall mingle more due to their high population density therefore increasing their chances of contracting the virus and dying. Hospitals and other medical facilities will have a period of being at full capacity 24/7, only allowing in those who are of high social class for they can pay more. A handsome amount of money shall be given as compensation to all surviving medical personnel. Most sick will die in their homes and the corpses shall be handed over to a unit for mass cremation. In a few months, all humans should have reaped the benefits of herd immunity, lower population densities, and with overpopulation and poverty problems gone, there would be infinite numbers of job opportunities open. This natural selection will also remove those who were either poor or indolent or have genes that compromise the immune system from the human population and strengthen the human gene pool.

This is my proposal, to let the virus spread like wildfire and let the flow of natural selection take place. These methods shall rid humanity of COVID-19 and many other plagues that man created in themselves. My proposal shall get us through this small bump in our timeline and help human civilization advance.
