About the Site

Team change is a (fictional) private organization and community established and headed by the (Also Fictional)(James Red Foundation. Our efforts are supported by a far-left community and our mission is to spread our practical idealism to the public. Now with our newly-launched website, we are reaching out to those who are unsatisfied, indifferent, and everyone uncontented with how things are being done.

With whatever radical or controversial opinions you may have that may not be taken too well by the general public, the team change site is open to all. We are always open-minded in terms of submissions for content to posted on the site. The Team Change official site will always be a safe place to post your ideas and possibly connect with people with the same mindsets. We post weekly on our official page on the latest news, updates, and plans for the activity of the organization. We will also post job offers, petitions, and polls regarding current issues to be addressed in the future. We also post our email links and our hotlines in the site to make sure that you know where to find us for submissions and other ordeals. Advocate for change now! Enjoy your visit.
